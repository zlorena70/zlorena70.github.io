About Me

Hello, I am


A Growing Career

Welcome to my page! If you follow my journey, you will immediately see that it is one of change, relentless drive, and positivity. A native of Miami, Florida, I began studying classical music at the age of three. I later attended the Florida State University College of Music, aiming to pursue a career as an opera singer and voice professor while simultaneously attending the College of Social Sciences and Public Policy to study international affairs and communications. Soon after graduating in 2013, however, I had to cut short my musical career for unexpected medical reasons. But I discovered a blossoming career in growth marketing and communications , where I rekindled my passion for connection and storytelling. Continuous learning is a great passion of mine and I sought new ways to increase skills such as: HTML/CSS, Google Analytics, Facebook Ads, Basic Graphic Design, SQL, Marketing Automation, GitHub, Email Marketing, and so much more.

If you're looking for a well-rounded, dedicated, and creative individual, don't hesitate to connect!
